An exploration of everyday life in Yorkist and early Tudor England

The 15th century was a period of enormous political, social, economic, and demographic upheaval for England. The ravaging of the country by war and plague in the previous century left the foundations of medieval English society weak and ripe for change. The characteristically medieval England of 1400 was fundamentally changed by 1500, readying the country for the emergence of modern society.

We are amateur historians whose research informs our personas in the Society for Creative Anachronism, a historical recreation group that focuses on recreating the life in the middle ages. Our personas are fictional, but based upon research on the historical Tunstall family.
